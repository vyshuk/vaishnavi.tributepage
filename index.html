About the Legend

.A.P.J Abdul kalam in full Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen abdul kalm ,was born in october 15,1931,in Rameshwaram Tamilnadu,india.

.He served as the 11th presedent of india from 2002 to 2007.

.kalam earned a degree in aeroautical engeeniring from the Madras institution of science and technology and in 1958 joined the Defence Research and Development Organisation(DRDO).

.In 1969,he moved to the Indian Space Research Organisation,where he was project director of the SLV-III, the first satellite lauch vehicle that was both designed and produced in India.

.Among those successes was Agni,India's first Intermediate-range ballistic missible,which incorporated aspects of the SLV-III was launched in 1989

.He also played a pivotal organisational,technical and polticalrole in indias Pokhran-II nuclear test in 1998,the first science the orginal nuclear test by india 1974,

.From 1992 to 1997 kalam was scientific advisor to the defence minister,he later served as principal scientific advisor (1999-2001) to the government with the rank of cabinet minister

.His prominent role in the country's 1998 nuclear weapons tests solidfied india as nuclear power and established kalm as a national hero,althrough the test caused great concern in the international community

.In 1998 kalam put forward a countrywide plan called Technology Vision 2020, which he described as a road map for transforming india from a less-developed a developed society in 20 years The plan called for,among other measures,incresing agricultural technology and widening access to health care and education.

.kalm received 7 honorary dectorates from 40 universities.The Government of India honoured him with the Padma Bhushan in 1981 and the Padma Vibushan in 1990 for his work with ISRO and DRDO and his role as a scitific advisor to the Government.

.In 2013 ,he was recepient of the Von Braun Award from the national space society "to recognize exllence in the management AND leadership of space -related project"
